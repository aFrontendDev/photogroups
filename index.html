Welcome to PhotoGroups!

Use PhotoGroups to create groups where you can all share your pictures. It's perfect when you have a big night out with friends, family get together, stag and hen parties, weddings, birthdays, etc.

Usually people upload their pictures to facebook, instagram, etc. This isn't always ideal for groups though, especially if you don't know everyone well enough that you're friends on facebook or you don't want to find someones instagram account just to see the pictures they took on your hen party!
